How It Works

One connected flow — quote to payment in five taps

Every step flows into the next. No re-entering data, no switching apps. Your whole business runs from one screen.

1
Quote the job
Select the client, pick the service, and the price auto-fills from your pricing matrix. Send a professional quote by text or email.
2
Client approves
Client taps approve with one click — no login, no app needed. You get notified instantly.
3
Schedule the job
Approved quote becomes a scheduled job instantly. Assign a cleaner and it appears on their calendar right away.
4
Worker completes
Your cleaner taps Arrived then Completed from their phone. You see real-time status without calling anyone.
5
Payment collected
Worker records cash, Venmo, Zelle, or card on-site. Receipt sent to the client automatically.
6
Invoice generated
A branded invoice is ready to download or send via WhatsApp, text, or email. One tap, done.
Features

Built for cleaning. Not adapted from plumbing software.

Eight tools that cover everything your cleaning business needs — from your first lead to your last payment of the day.

AI booking agent
Your business answers leads 24/7 — even at 9pm on a Saturday. The AI agent takes inquiries by phone, SMS, or web, checks your availability, quotes a price, and books the job. Included on all plans.
Smart scheduling
Recurring jobs, team calendars, and worker assignment. When someone calls in sick, see who's available and reassign in seconds.
Instant quoting
Select the client, pick the service, and the price auto-fills from your pricing matrix. Send a professional quote by text or email. Client approves with one tap.
Invoicing + PDF
Create invoices from completed jobs with one click. Download branded PDFs and send via WhatsApp, text, or email.
On-site payment collection
Worker finishes the job, taps Complete, and records the payment — cash, Venmo, Zelle, card. Receipt sent automatically.
Client profiles with property details
Alarm codes, key access, parking instructions, pet details — everything your cleaners need, right on their job card. No more calling the office.
English + Spanish
Your whole team uses the app in the language they're comfortable with. Full bilingual interface, switchable anytime.
Works on any device
Phone, tablet, or computer. No app store download needed — just open your browser.
