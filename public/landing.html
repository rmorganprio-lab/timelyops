The Problem

You started a service business,
not a paperwork business.

You're great at what you do — cleaning, plumbing, landscaping, whatever your craft. But tracking jobs in your head, quoting on napkins, chasing payments by text, and managing your team from a spreadsheet? That's not why you started this business. TimelyOps puts it all in one place so you can focus on the work that matters.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Six powerful tools that cover your daily operations from morning to night.

Smart Scheduling
Calendar with team view. See who's working where, assign jobs, and manage recurring visits — weekly, biweekly, monthly.
Instant Quotes
Auto-fill pricing from your rate card. Create professional quotes in under 2 minutes, for existing clients or brand new prospects.
One-Tap Check-In
Workers tap "Arrived" and "Completed" right from their phone. You see real-time status of every job — no phone calls needed.
Payment Tracking
Collect payment on-site immediately after job completion — Cash, Venmo, Zelle, Card. Partial payments handled automatically.
PDF Invoices
Generate branded PDF invoices with one tap. Send via WhatsApp, text, or email. Completed jobs auto-create invoices.
Team Management
Role-based access — owners see everything, workers see only their jobs. Morning dashboard shows your full team's schedule at a glance.
How It Works

From quote to cash in four steps.

A connected pipeline that flows naturally from start to finish.

1
Quote the job
Select the client, pick a service, and prices auto-fill. Send a professional quote in under 2 minutes.
2
Schedule the work
Client approves? One tap turns the quote into a job on the calendar with a worker assigned.
3
Complete the job
Worker taps Arrived, does the work, taps Completed. Immediately asked "Did you receive payment?"
4
Get paid
Payment recorded on-site or invoice sent automatically. PDF invoices downloaded and shared in one tap.
